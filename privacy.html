Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Welcome to ERRIHO Portal. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and financial data in compliance with the Philippines' Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identity: Full Name and Account Reference Number.
  • Financial Data: Loan principal, interest rates, payment history, outstanding balances, and credit ratings.
  • Transaction Records: Reference numbers, dates of payment, and late penalty records.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with a real-time dashboard of your Flavi Deal installment plans.
  • To calculate and display your current credit rating and "pay my due" obligations.
  • To facilitate secure redirects to our payment gateways.
  • Automated Notifications: We use your registered mobile number, email address, and Messenger account to send official billing statements, payment confirmations, and automated reminders regarding your account status.

3. Data Storage (LocalStorage)

As seen in our system architecture, we use LocalStorage to keep your session active. This data is stored locally on your device and is automatically cleared upon logout or after 1 minute of inactivity to ensure your security.

4. Data Sharing

ERRIHO Personal Finance does not sell or share your data with outside marketing agencies. Data is only shared internally with our partner, Flavi Deal, for account synchronization.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access your financial statement, request corrections for any discrepancies, and withdraw consent by closing your account once all obligations are settled.
